About CloakID

Privacy-first phone protection for everyone

Our Mission

CloakID keeps your real phone number private while ensuring important calls always get through. We believe your personal phone number is sensitive information that shouldn't be shared with everyone who asks for it.

Founded in 2024 by Niobium LLC, CloakID gives you real phone numbers for different parts of your life — work, dating, selling online, or anything else — so your personal number stays protected.

Our AI-powered call screening stops spam before it rings, while our Handshake trust system ensures the people who matter always get through.

Why CloakID?

Real Protection

Each persona gets a real, carrier-grade phone number. This isn't call forwarding or virtual voicemail — it's actual phone numbers that work for calls and texts.

AI-Powered Screening

Our AI screens every incoming call using multiple verification sources. Spam is stopped before it rings. Legitimate callers get through. You stay in control.

Trust System

The Handshake system lets you create a trusted inner circle. These contacts bypass all screening and always reach you immediately.

Who Uses CloakID

CloakID is for anyone who values their privacy:

Parents

Keep a separate number for school, activities, and services. Protect your family from unwanted calls while staying reachable for what matters.

Dating

Don't give out your real number until you're ready. Use a dating persona and upgrade contacts when trust is earned.

Online Sellers

Selling on Craigslist or Marketplace? Use a dedicated number. When the transaction is complete, you're done.

Consultants

Separate client calls from personal life. Give each project its own number. When the engagement ends, so does the number.

Healthcare Workers

Keep work calls separate from personal time. Use dedicated personas for work communication without exposing your personal number.

Legal Professionals

Maintain confidentiality with separate numbers for different matters. Control who can reach you and when.
