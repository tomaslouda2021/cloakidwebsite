Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any differences.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and ACH transfers for Enterprise accounts. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for any plan. You can start using CloakID immediately after signing up, and your 14-day free trial begins right away.

What happens after my trial?

After your 14-day trial, you'll be automatically enrolled in your chosen plan. We'll send reminders before your trial ends so you can make an informed decision.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! We offer 20% off for annual billing on all plans. Non-profits and educational institutions qualify for additional discounts.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new customers. If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days of your paid subscription, we'll refund your payment in full.
