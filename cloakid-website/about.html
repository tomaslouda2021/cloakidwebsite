About CloakID

Professional identity management for the modern workplace

Our Mission

Niobium LLC is a digital identity management platform that helps professionals organize their communications across multiple contexts. Our AI-powered system enables users to maintain appropriate boundaries between work, personal, and professional relationships while staying connected and accessible.

Founded in 2024, CloakID serves consultants, healthcare professionals, legal practitioners, real estate agents, and business executives who need to manage multiple professional identities efficiently.

Our platform provides account management services, automated notifications, and intelligent communication routing to enhance professional productivity and maintain appropriate business boundaries.

Why CloakID?

Professional Focus

Built specifically for business professionals who need to manage multiple roles and relationships without compromising their personal boundaries.

AI-Powered Intelligence

Our smart system learns your communication patterns and helps you stay organized automatically, saving hours of manual sorting.

Enterprise Security

Bank-level encryption and compliance features ensure your professional communications remain secure and confidential.
