Delete Your Account

CloakID by Niobium LLC

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the CloakID app. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the CloakID app on your device
  2. Tap the menu icon (top-left corner) to open the side menu
  3. Tap Profile
  4. Scroll down to the Danger Zone section
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Read the warning and tap Continue
  7. Type DELETE to confirm, then tap Delete Forever

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered:

  • Your user account and profile information
  • All personas and their associated phone numbers
  • All call history and call logs
  • All messages (SMS and MMS)
  • All contacts and approved callers
  • All handshake (trusted caller) contacts and invitations
  • Push notification settings and device tokens
  • Voicemail recordings

Data Retention

No user data is retained after account deletion. All phone numbers associated with your personas are immediately released back to the telephony provider and cannot be recovered. This action is permanent and irreversible.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance deleting your account, contact us at support@cloakid.app with the email address associated with your account, and we will process your request.
