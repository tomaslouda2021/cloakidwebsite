Communication preferences.

What text messages CloakID sends, why we send them, and how to opt out.

SMS Opt-in Disclosure

By creating a CloakID account, you agree to receive text messages from Niobium LLC at the phone number you provide. These messages include:

  • Account verification and setup confirmations
  • Service notifications and feature announcements
  • Billing reminders and payment confirmations
  • Technical support and customer service messages
  • Security alerts and important account information

Message frequency varies based on your account activity. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt-out, reply STOP to any message or contact support@cloakid.app.

For help, reply HELP or contact support@cloakid.app.

Types of Messages You May Receive

Account Setup Messages

When you create your CloakID account, you'll receive:

  • Welcome message with setup instructions
  • Verification codes for account security
  • Confirmation of persona creation
  • Initial configuration assistance

Service Notifications

To keep you informed about your CloakID service:

  • New feature announcements
  • System maintenance notifications
  • Service updates and improvements
  • Tips for using CloakID effectively

Billing Communications

Related to your subscription and payments:

  • Payment confirmations
  • Renewal reminders
  • Failed payment notifications
  • Subscription changes

Security Alerts

To protect your account:

  • Login from new device notifications
  • Password change confirmations
  • Suspicious activity alerts
  • Two-factor authentication codes

Calling Features

Related to voice calling:

  • Relay call setup confirmations
  • Missed call notifications
  • Voicemail alerts

Number Lifecycle

Related to the phone numbers attached to your personas:

  • Temporary persona expiry warnings (48 hours, 24 hours, and 1 hour before)
  • Number release confirmations
  • Upgrade and swap confirmations

Managing Your Communication Preferences

Opt-Out Options

You can opt-out of SMS messages at any time through multiple methods:

  • Text Reply: Reply STOP to any text message from CloakID
  • In-App Settings: Update your communication preferences in the CloakID app
  • Email Support: Contact support@cloakid.app to update your preferences

Opt-In Again

If you've opted out and wish to receive messages again:

  • Text START to the CloakID number
  • Update preferences in the CloakID app
  • Contact our support team at support@cloakid.app

Message Frequency and Charges

  • Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your account activity and the types of notifications you've enabled.
  • Charges: Standard message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan.
  • No Premium Charges: CloakID does not charge premium rates for SMS messages.

Privacy and Security

Your phone number and communication preferences are protected according to our Privacy Policy. We:

  • Never sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing
  • Use secure systems to send messages
  • Only send messages related to your CloakID account and services
  • Comply with all applicable messaging regulations

Messaging is user-initiated and limited to direct, one-to-one conversations.

Supported Carriers

CloakID SMS messages are supported by all major U.S. carriers including but not limited to:

  • AT&T
  • Verizon
  • T-Mobile
  • U.S. Cellular
  • And most MVNO carriers

Help and Support

If you need assistance with SMS messages or communication preferences:

Text: Reply HELP to any CloakID message

Email: support@cloakid.app

In-App: Visit Support in the CloakID app side menu

Terms and Conditions

SMS services are provided as part of your CloakID account and are subject to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. By opting in to receive SMS messages, you confirm that:

  • You are the authorized user of the phone number provided
  • You consent to receive automated text messages
  • You understand that message and data rates may apply
  • You can opt-out at any time
