What Happens When Someone Calls

1

Caller is asked to identify themselves

When someone dials your persona number, they hear a brief prompt asking who they are and why they're calling.

2

The call is screened

Based on what the caller says, your persona's purpose, and your screening rules, the call is evaluated in seconds.

3

You get the result

Legitimate calls ring your phone. Suspicious calls go to voicemail. Spam is blocked. You see everything in your activity feed with a full record of what happened.
