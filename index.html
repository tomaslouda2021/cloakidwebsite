How CloakID protects you

Built for calm, not noise.

Multiple personas

Create a persona for each part of your life. Work, dating, side hustle, deliveries — each gets its own real US number.

Calls screened first

Every unknown caller is asked who they are and why they're calling. Only the ones that make sense for that persona get through.

End-to-end encrypted

Your calls, messages, and contacts are encrypted at rest and in transit. We can't read them. Neither can anyone else.

Temporary numbers

Selling a couch on Craigslist? Spin up a 7-day disposable number. When it expires, the number is released — no trail.

Real SMS & voicemail

Every persona sends and receives real text messages, voicemail, and even makes outbound calls with its own caller ID.

Your real number stays private

No one you call from CloakID ever sees your real number. Ever. Not dealerships, not delivery drivers, not strangers.

Questions

The short answers.

Are these real phone numbers?

Yes. Every CloakID persona comes with a real US phone number that can send and receive real calls and SMS — nothing virtual, nothing fake.

Does CloakID work with iMessage?

CloakID sends and receives standard SMS/MMS over the carrier network. You can message anyone, on any device.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play. Cancel anytime, no questions asked.

What happens when I delete my account?

Everything is permanently deleted — personas, call logs, messages, voicemail, contacts. Phone numbers are immediately released back to the provider. See our account deletion page.
