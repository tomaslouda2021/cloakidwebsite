Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during the free trial?

You get full access to CloakID for 14 days. No credit card required. Try everything — AI screening, real phone numbers, the Handshake trust system — and decide if it's right for you.

What happens after the trial ends?

If you don't subscribe after trial, persona numbers are deactivated and calls won't route. Your data is preserved for 30 days in case you decide to subscribe later.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime. Trial is free — no charge during trial. If you're charged unexpectedly, contact support and we'll make it right.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through Stripe. Subscriptions are also available through the iOS App Store and Google Play.

What does "unlimited SMS" mean?

CloakID is designed for personal privacy, not bulk or automated messaging. Normal personal use is unlimited. Unusual volume may be throttled to prevent abuse.

Do you offer team or enterprise plans?

CloakID currently supports individual users. Team features may be introduced in the future. Contact us if you have specific needs.
