Quick Start Guide

From download to your first protected call in under five minutes.

1

Download the app

CloakID is available on the App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android). Create your account with your phone number — it stays private and is never shared.

2

Create your first persona

Tap "Create Persona" and choose a name and area code. CloakID assigns a real phone number that you can give out instead of your personal number.

3

Set up call screening

Tell CloakID who you expect to call this number and what it's used for. Incoming calls are screened based on your preferences — only relevant callers get through.

4

Share your persona number

Give out your persona number via QR code, SMS, or vCard. Use it for signups, business contacts, dating, deliveries — anything where you'd rather not share your real number.

5

Manage your contacts

Add contacts to your personas so you know who's calling. Approved callers are recognized automatically and routed based on your preferences.

Troubleshooting

Calls aren't coming through

Check that call screening is enabled for your persona. If using intake mode, calls go directly to voicemail by design. Also make sure push notifications are enabled so you're alerted to incoming calls.

SMS not sending or receiving

Make sure you have an active subscription and your persona number is still active (not expired). If you're on a temporary persona, check that it hasn't passed its 7-day expiry. Restart the app if the issue persists.

Biometric login not working

If Face ID or Touch ID stops working, try disabling and re-enabling biometric auth in Settings. If you changed your device biometrics (added a new face or fingerprint), you'll need to re-authenticate with your password first.

Legitimate callers being screened out

Update your persona's context description to better reflect who you expect to call. For example, if you use a persona for a business, include the types of callers you expect (customers, delivery services, suppliers). You can also add specific callers as approved contacts so they're recognized automatically.

iOS widget showing stale data

Open the CloakID app and navigate to the Personas tab — this triggers a widget refresh. If the widget still shows old data, remove it from your home screen and re-add it.
