How CloakID Protects You

Call Routing

Incoming calls are routed based on your preferences before they reach you. Caller information is shown to help you decide who gets through.

Real Phone Numbers

Each persona gets a real phone number. Your personal number stays hidden. Make calls and send texts showing your persona number, never your real one.

Handshake Trust System

Create a trusted contacts list that you control. Approved contacts are routed according to your preferences. Invitation-based access lets you accept or decline new contacts.

Built For People Who Value Privacy

Parents

Protect your family from unwanted calls. Give out a separate number for school, activities, and services. Keep your personal line for people who matter.

Dating

Keep your real number private until you're ready to share it. Use a dating persona and upgrade contacts to your real number when trust is earned.

Online Sellers

Selling on Craigslist, Facebook Marketplace, or classified ads? Use a dedicated persona. When the sale is done, you're done — no lingering calls.

Consultants & Freelancers

Separate client calls from personal life. Give each client or project its own number. When the engagement ends, so does the number.

Healthcare Professionals

Keep work calls separate from personal time. Use dedicated personas for patient communication without exposing your personal number.

Legal Practitioners

Maintain confidentiality with separate numbers for different matters. Control who can reach you and when.
