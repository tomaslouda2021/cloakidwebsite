The problem

Your number is the most reused password you'll ever have.

You hand the same ten digits to every form, dealership, dating app, and delivery driver. From there it spreads to data brokers, lead lists, and spam dialers — and once it's out, there is no way to take it back.

Three steps to a quieter phone.

Set it up once. CloakID handles the rest in the background.

01

Create a persona

Pick an area code and get a real US number in seconds. Name it Work, Dating, Deliveries — whatever fits.

02

Security

Built to forget.

A privacy product is only as good as what it refuses to keep. We designed CloakID so there's as little to lose as possible.

Encrypted at rest & in transit

Your phone numbers, call recordings, and voicemail are encrypted. Screening runs automatically — no human at CloakID listens to your calls — and call audio is deleted once it's handled.

Never sold, never shared

We don't sell your data, share it with brokers, or build an advertising profile. That's not our business model — your subscription is.

Delete and walk away

Close your account and your personas, call logs, and messages are deleted from CloakID — and your numbers are released the same day.

Questions

Are these real phone numbers?

Yes. Every CloakID persona comes with a real US phone number that can place and receive real calls and SMS — nothing virtual, nothing fake.

Does CloakID work with iMessage?

CloakID sends and receives standard SMS/MMS over the carrier network, so you can message anyone on any device.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play. Cancel anytime, no questions asked.

What happens when I delete my account?
