Subscription details

CloakID Core ($9.99/month) and CloakID Executive ($19.99/month) are auto-renewable subscriptions billed through your Apple ID. A 7-day free trial is offered to new subscribers.

Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Your subscription automatically renews for the same period and price unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account is charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.

You can manage or cancel your subscription in your App Store account settings after purchase. By subscribing you agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Questions

Pricing FAQ.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime through the App Store subscription settings. If the trial is active, you won't be charged.

What happens to my numbers if I cancel?

Your phone numbers are released at the end of your billing period. Number releases are permanent — you can't get the same number back.

Do you offer team or enterprise plans?

Not yet. CloakID is built for individuals today. Contact us if you have team needs — we'd love to hear about them.

What payment methods do you accept?

Billing runs through the App Store — any payment method you have set up with Apple works. Google Play is coming soon; join the Android waitlist to hear when it's ready.
