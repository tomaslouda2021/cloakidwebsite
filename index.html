Take your number off the table.

Real US numbers you can hand out, route, and retire. The one that reaches your family stays off the internet.

Every vendor, every form, every court record, every data broker treats your phone number as a public identifier. Once it's out, it stays out. CloakID returns the one-to-one: one number per counterparty, one line nobody else has.

  • Separate numbers, separate risk. Give each counterparty a line that only reaches them. Retire it without consequence.

  • Your data is not for sale. No ad networks. No brokers. No partner pipelines.
